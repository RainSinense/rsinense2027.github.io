Stages Of Food Processing

Crushing Stage

The first place our food arrives in is of course our mouth. Inside our mouth is the saliva, which helps in lubricating the food we intake to which also helps in our digestion. After the food gets lubricated inside the mouth, it then goes through the esophagus until it reaches the stomach. The stomach then breaks down the food with the gastric acid present inside it.

Trainsfer Stage

Now, after our body digests the food we eat, our body will finally excrete the excess wastes left in our body. While excreting these wastes, our body also excretes out the waste fluids in our body. Similar to the solid wastes in our body, these waste fluids that goes inside our body has to go though absorption so that our body would be able to absorb the nutrients in it. While we excrete these wastes, there can still be extra wastes that can be left out inside our body which is why it is professionally removed in some instances. This excretion of solid wastes out of our body can only be done in our anus and nowhere else. Finally, after these cycles, the wastes will finally reach the outside of our body aka the “outside world”.
